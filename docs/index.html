Everything You Need for School IDs

From student sync to card printing, we've got you covered.

FACTS SIS Sync

Automatically import students and employees from FACTS SIS. Keep your data in sync with scheduled updates.

ID Card Generation

Choose from 12+ card designs. Export to PDF for bulk printing. Customize with your school branding.

Photo Management

Upload, crop, and store photos in S3-compatible storage. Pull photos directly from FACTS SIS.

QR Verification

Every ID card includes a QR code linking to a public verification page. Instantly validate student and employee status.

Google Workspace

Optional OAuth login, Gmail signature management, and automatic photo sync to Google Workspace profiles.

Self-Hosted & Private

Deploy on your own infrastructure. Your student data never leaves your control. No subscriptions, no vendor lock-in.
